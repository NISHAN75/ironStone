What is EOS®?

The Entrepreneurial Operating System®, or EOS®, is a revolutionary methodology that empowers business owners like you to confidently tackle complex issues, supercharge leadership skills, and seize your ambitions with precision and clarity.

MEET

Andrea Schlapia

Business Owner | Educator | Coach | Speaker

Andrea offers clients a high-energy blend of professional expertise, personal warmth, and a passion for making a difference. In addition to holding designations as a Certified EOS Implementer®, Registered Corporate Coach (RCC), a Human Capital Strategist (HCS), and a Strategic HR Business Partner (sHRBP), Andrea is a Certified Administrator of Working Genius, PXT Select™, and Myers-Briggs assessments.

Andrea’s 25+ years of experience in financial services led her to become a successful financial advisor, coach, and mentor to leadership teams and business owners. As the founding owner of Ironstone, her nationally known practice management firm, Andrea has been featured in multiple industry publications, podcasts, and speaking engagements.

Now, imagine transforming your team with an accountability partner to lead you through the process!
